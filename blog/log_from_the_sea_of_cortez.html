Chapter None

Welcome to the blog from the Sea of Cortez! This page is dedicated to Steinbeck and Ed Ricket's account from their voyage on the Sea of Cortez. Use the navigation to flip through the chapters and journey with the Western Flyer and its crew. For my project, I picked a quote I found meaningful, funny, or otherwise signifigant from every chapter as a way to represent the journey readers take with Steinbeck and the crew.

Credit:

The quotes used throughout this project are from the Log from the Sea of Cortez. The specific format I used was an Apple Books text available from: John Steinbeck & Richard Astro. “The Log from the Sea of Cortez.” Apple Books. https://books.apple.com/us/book/the-log-from-the-sea-of-cortez/id357996728
